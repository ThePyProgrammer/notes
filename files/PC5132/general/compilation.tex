\subsection{Rydberg's Formula}

$$\frac{1}\lambda = R_\infty\left(\frac{1}{m^2} - \frac{1}{n^2} \right) \quad \implies \quad \lambda = \frac{m^2n^2}{(n^2-m^2)R_\infty}$$

Here $R_\infty$ is the Rydberg Constant.
